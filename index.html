About Us

Christ's Advocate Christian Ministry ay itinatag upang magdala ng pag-asa, gabay, at bibliya-based na pagtuturo sa Bulacan. Layunin naming maglingkod sa komunidad sa pamamagitan ng pagsamba, discipleship, at outreach programs.

Mission: To proclaim Jesus Christ, disciple believers, and serve our community with compassion.

Services & Ministries

Sunday Worship

Traditional & contemporary worship gatherings where we preach the Word and enjoy corporate praise.

Bible Study / Discipleship

Weekly small groups for studying Scripture and growing together in faith.

Children & Youth

Age-appropriate ministries that teach children and youth about Jesus in creative ways.

Community Outreach

Feeding programs, assistance drives, and practical help for neighbors in need.
